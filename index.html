About

I am a second year Masters student of Computer Science at UCLA. Currently, I'm working with Prof. Karen McKinnon on developing a deep learning model for longlead heatwave predictions. Please see my Active Projects for a more detailed overview of the research. Previously, I received my Bachelors in Applied Mathematics from Northwestern University where I focused on analytical solutions and computational approximations of differential equations. My interests lie in Artificial Intelligence and Predictive Modeling. When I'm not cleaning data, I enjoy climbing, trail running, and backpacking around the Sierras.
